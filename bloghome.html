House of Leaves: An Overview

After recently reading HoL, I had some thoughts I thought I'd share.

While Danielewski himself classifies House of Leaves as a horror story, I can't help but think that the core of it all might just be love. At least in the Navidson Record. The story of Johnny Truant that we see through the footnotes not only provides a lovely counter-vibe to Zampano's lengthy (and often tiresome) analysis of the so-called documentary, but gives us a riveting secondary (though I hesitate to call it such) story to follow.
